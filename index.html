Bromine from Total Chlorine Reading

Converts DPD Total Chlorine reading to Total Bromine. Pass/fail vs. TB MED 575 standards.

mg/L
📋 TB MED 575 §2-29 — Bromine Standards
  • Indoor Pool (min): 3.0 mg/L
  • Indoor Pool (max): 4.0 mg/L
  • Spa (min): 6.0 mg/L
  • Outdoor Pool: Not permitted (UV degrades bromine)
  • Conversion: TC (DPD) × 2.25 = Total Bromine
  • Feed method: Solid-stick or tablet-type, NSF-certified equipment

Langelier Saturation Index (LSI)

Determines corrosive or scale-forming tendency of pool water. Target: –0.5 to +0.5.

TB MED 575 Appendix G
📋 TB MED 575 Appendix G — LSI Standards
  • Formula: LSI = pH + TF + CF + AF − 12.1
  • Acceptable range: −0.5 to +0.5
  • Negative value: Corrosive tendency
  • Positive value: Scale-forming tendency
  • pH target: 7.4 – 7.6
  • Total Alkalinity: 50 – 150 mg/L
